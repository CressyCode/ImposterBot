Ambush

Information

Our homemade bot can run a turn-based strategy game!

Ambush your friends by using the command !ambush @user

If you need help or notice bugs, ping @Khazaari#1515, @Cressy#4851, or @MoustachioMario#2067.

Commands

If you're still confused, feel free to ask questions!

  • Let's say you have a Werewolf in your first slot, and your opponent has a Dragon in their second slot, in order to make your Werewolf attack their Dragon you would type attack 1 2
  • If you have a Werewolf in your first slot with the ability “Terrorize”. Using your turn, you can swap "Terrorize" to it's other ability, “Solid Defense", by typing swap 1.
  • To refresh the ambush embed to the bottom of the channel, send: r, !r, or refresh
  • To end an active ambush battle, send: end
